About Me

As a professional with an MBA and a strong background in management, I bring a unique blend of strategic thinking and technical proficiency to software development. My passion is creating functional and visually engaging applications that solve real-world problems. I am a quick and independent learner, as demonstrated by my certifications in full-stack JavaScript and cybersecurity from IBM and Google.

I excel in remote environments, leveraging strong communication, adaptability, and problem-solving skills to ensure seamless collaboration and high-quality results. I am committed to building impactful software solutions that are both robust and aesthetically pleasing.
